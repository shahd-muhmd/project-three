Populer destinations in Egypt

Major tourist destinations include the millennia-old monuments in the Nile Valley. Principal among them are the Pyramids and Great Sphinx at Giza.

Luxor is famous for its temples, tombs, and the Valley of the Kings, making it a prime tourist destination in Egypt.

The Sinai Peninsula is known for its stunning beaches, mountains, and religious significance, including Mount Sinai and St. Catherine's Monastery.
